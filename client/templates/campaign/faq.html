FAQ General questions

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Techconnectr is only required. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

What is the currency used for all transactions?

All prices for themes, templates and other items, including each seller's or buyer's account balance are in USD

How does Techconnectr guarantee the quality of my leads?

All leads are processed by Techconnector's dynamic lead quality and integrity evaluation. To ensure all Techconnector Portal LeadFlow campaigns receive the requested volume of quality leads, Techconnector delivers an additional 10% of leads with each campaign to account for any potential data concerns once the campaign reaches the lead goal.

Can agencies use the Techconnectr Portal on behalf of their clients?

Yes, the Techconnectr Portal is available to all professionals and offers an exclusive program for agencies. The Agency Advantage Program rewards agencies for running their clients' content syndication lead generation campaigns with the Techconnectr Portal. Agencies receive an invitation to the program with more detailed information once their account is created.
